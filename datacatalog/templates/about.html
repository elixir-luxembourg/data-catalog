Background

The {{ config.get('INSTANCE_TITLE', 'Data Catalogue') }} provides a centralized metadata repository for biomedical datasets, supporting data discovery, reuse, and compliance. It enables researchers to explore study descriptions across disease areas and access curated metadata from diverse projects and sources.

Evolution

Initially launched as the Translational Data Catalogue under IMI and H2020 initiatives jointly developed through ELIXIR Luxembourg, IMI-FAIRplus and IMI-eTRIKS collaborations. The catalogue has evolved into a broader service provided by ELIXIR Luxembourg and while IMI projects remain foundational, the catalogue now supports a wider range of datasets from national and international research efforts.

Unique Value

The catalogue centralizes metadata from large-scale biomedical research projects, improving findability and promoting FAIR data principles. It serves as a gateway for researchers to discover datasets and understand their context, provenance, and access conditions.

Approach

Developed through lean, user-centered design, the catalogue integrates community-accepted metadata models and supports diverse data types. It continues to grow through contributions from ELIXIR-LU and partner projects.

Impact

The catalogue enhances visibility and accessibility of biomedical datasets, fostering collaboration and accelerating research. It supports data sharing, compliance, and reproducibility across the research ecosystem.
